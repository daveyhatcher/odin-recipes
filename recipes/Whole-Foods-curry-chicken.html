Whole Foods Curry Chicken Salad

Here's a copycat version of the delicious Whole Foods Curry Chicken Salad.

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Dried Currants
  • Sliced Almonds
  • Curry Powder
  • Mango Chutney
  • Cooked Chicken

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine all the ingredients.
  2. Stir to combine and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Serve by itself or on lettuce.
